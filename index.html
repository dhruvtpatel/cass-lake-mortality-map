Minnesota death rates by area, 2011–2024

Population-adjusted. Each circle is a ZIP-code area (597 areas; institutional/denominator artifacts removed); size and color show the rate, not the raw count. Cass Lake / St. Regis Superfund site marked in red.

  Statewide views are crude per-capita rates (older areas read somewhat higher); the neighbors view is age-adjusted. Dioxin-related = lung, liver, kidney, soft-tissue sarcoma, lymphoma & leukemia.
